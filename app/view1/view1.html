About this Site

This data provides a window into how people are interacting with the government online. The data comes from a unified Google Analytics account for City of Los Angeles webpages created by the Mayor's Data Team. This program helps government agencies understand how people find, access, and use government services online. The program does not track individuals, and anonymizes the IP addresses of visitors.

We plan to expand the data made available here. If you have any suggestions, or spot any issues or bugs, please contact the Mayor's Data Team.
